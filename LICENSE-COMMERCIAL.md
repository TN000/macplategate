- **Free / open-source use:** [AGPL-3.0](LICENSE). You can self-host, modify and run MacPlateGate at no cost. Any modifications you distribute must be released under the AGPL-3.0, including server-side use that exposes the modified app to network users.

The free AGPL-3.0 version is fully functional — there is no feature gating between the two licenses. Commercial licensing is purely a legal / support arrangement.

## When you need a commercial license

You probably need one if any of the following apply:

- You are deploying MacPlateGate at multiple sites and want to keep your local modifications private.
- You want vendor-backed email support or installation help.
- You are integrating MacPlateGate into a commercial product or service offered to third parties.
- Your organization's policy prohibits using AGPL-licensed software.
